        <div>Our lab welcomes applications from prospective graduate students who are interested in perception and cognition and who have a background in, or strong motivation to learn, deep learning and artificial intelligence (AI). Prospective applicants are strongly encouraged to first participate in our research internship program to better understand the lab’s research topics and working environment. Applicants should submit their CV, transcript, and a brief research statement by email.</div>
